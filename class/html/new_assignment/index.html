WHY SDI-B?

Skill Development Institute, Bhubaneswar (SDI-B) is a flagship project of Oil & Gas PSUs established for imparting job skill training to unemployed & underprivileged youth of society to make them job ready. The institute is managed by IndianOil and registered as not-for-profit society.

SDI-B has been developed as a mega model skill institute with the sole objective of ‘Skilling Youth, Enhancing Livelihoods’. SDI-B’s five tenets are the following

  • Cutting-edge Infrastructure that sets new standards.

    A fully residential campus with separate hostels for boys and girls, academic block, workshop, recreation block, open-air-theatre, sports ground, faculty block, staff dormitory, auditorium, admin block and guesthouse, equipped with state-of-art facilities like smart classrooms, Wi-Fi access, digital learning platform etc.

  • Dive into the real world with Industry-Relevant Labs

    Centres of Excellence in partnership with leading industry players like Schneider, Siemens, Kemppi, CISCO and Tata STRIVE. Offers courses aligned with demands of industry. Candidates can join basic level to advanced level short-term courses (3 to 6 months) in manufacturing, CRM, product lifecycle management, energy management, solar PV, IT/ITeS, hospitality etc.

  • Unlock your career potential with a Higher Placement Rate.

    An outcome driven institute with greater emphasis on hands-on training covering both hard and soft skills so as to make students employable or enterprising ones to become entrepreneurs. The institute has consistently achieved placement success of 90% and above since inception.

  • Embark on a journey of Personal Growth through Holistic Learning.

    Our institute offers a comprehensive approach to learning that cultivates personal growth by integrating various dimensions of knowledge and skill development.

  • Access Quality Education at Nominal Fees, making excellence affordable for all.

    As a CSR project of Oil & Gas PSUs, our institute is committed to offering courses with a reasonable fee structure, striving to make quality education accessible to all segments of society.

Besides training of unemployed youth, SDI-B also organizes programs like training of trainers, corporate training programs, internship training to engineering students etc. leveraging institute’s excellent infrastructure.
